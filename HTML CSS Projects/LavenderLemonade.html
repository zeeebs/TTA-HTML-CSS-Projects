Lavendar Lemonade Recipe

Ingredients

- 6 cups water (divided)
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons dried lavender
- Â¾ cup fresh lemon juice (about 7 lemons)

Instructions

Lavender Simple Syrup

1. Bring 2 cups of water to boil. Remove from heat and pour over sugar. Stir until dissolved.
2. Stir in culinary grade dried lavender buds and let steep for at least 1 hour, leaving longer for a stronger flavor.

Lemonade

1. Combine lemon juice from sqeezed lemon and lavender simple syrup in a pitcher or other container.
2. Optional - Add remaining 4 cups of water.
*Note: For a stronger flavor, leave undiluted*
